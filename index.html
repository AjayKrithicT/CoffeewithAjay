About us Why choose us?

what makes our coffee special!?

Ajay Krithic, an engineer turned barista, proudly owns "Ajay's Cafe" in all over India. Fusing precision from his engineering background with a passion for coffee, Ajay curates a menu of diverse and exquisite blends, from velvety lattes to robust espressos. The cafe's warm ambiance and eclectic decor invite patrons to savor each sip amidst a cozy atmosphere. What sets Ajay's Cafe apart is Ajay himself — the owner and head barista — offering a personal touch to every customer interaction. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or casual drinker, Ajay's Cafe promises a memorable experience, celebrating the art and joy of a perfectly brewed cup.

Our Menu Popular Menu

Espresso

Espresso is made by forcing nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans, which results in a concentrated, syrup-like coffee drink.

₹175/-

Americano

The Americano is another popular type of coffee drink, and it's very easy to make! It's just espresso with hot water dripping over it.

₹200/-

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are a classic Italian type of coffee drink made of espresso, steamed milk, and thick foam in equal parts. The name comes from the resemblance to the hoods worn by Capuchin monks.

₹290/-

Macchiato

The word "macchiato" means spotted, so a macchiato espresso is an espresso with a splash of milk. Most coffee shops will top off this type of coffee drink with some foamed milk instead of just steamed milk for what's known as a traditional macchiato.

₹265/-

Cafe au Lait

Cafe au lait is a coffee beverage that is made with dark roast coffee and steamed milk.

₹230/-

Irish Coffee

One of the most popular drinks in Ireland is undoubtedly Irish coffee. This hot beverage is made with coffee, whiskey, and cream, and it is usually served with a spoonful of sugar to balance out the drink's bitterness.

₹300/-
